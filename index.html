<3's learning and cats, dreams in code

But on a slightly more serious note...

Initially I studied Front End Development with the hopes of simply rejuvenating my Graphic Design Diploma. I never anticipated how thoroughly I would get sucked into solving problems with my new-found coding skills. I’ve enjoyed and struggled through everything from dev ops related errors, version control and learning how to contribute to a team repo with git, discovering the simplicity of Ruby on Rails, delving into Rails MVC and writing and fixing my own basic rspec tests.

I also found that I love sharing my knowledge with others as much as I enjoy expanding my own skill set, and have found tutoring young girls coding with Code4CT and introducing the Railsbridge course to newcomers extremely extremely rewarding.

What else can I tell you about myself? Well, I have an insatiable desire to learn (either on my own or under guidance), a strong and committed work ethic, no fear of working in new frameworks or new languages, a willingness to struggle through the unknown, an eye for layout, the capability to create my own artwork, mock-ups and hi-fidelity designs, a love of technology and the internet and a passion for sharing what I’ve learnt with others.
